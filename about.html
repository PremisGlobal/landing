Introducing the Team

Erik Chen / Tech Lead

Erik is a graduate of UC Berkeley and has backend software engineering experience building scalable online advertising solutions for merchants at eBay Advertising. A Bay Area native, he is familiar with the entrepreneurial spirit of the Valley and best practices of tech development. He has a passion for delivering value to customers and being a well rounded contributor to every aspect of the business.

Steven To / Founder

Steven is a multi national citizen who has studied and lived in Hong Kong, England, Los Angeles, and the Silicon Valley. Coming from a large family well versed in business and trade, Steven has always had an innate knack for foreseeing the next big opportunity. Studying at USC also gives Steven access to many talented and ambitious entrepreneurs and developers. With his easy going humor, Steven hopes to provide the necessary spark to lead this team to build the next big thing.

Charley Chen / Mobile Developer

Charley is a graduate of UCLA with both back end and mobile development experience. He has previous experience with launching the Android app Shareful for Kindred Labs, a social good startup based in downtown Los Angeles. He has also worked on the Infrastructre team of AWS at Amazon in Seattle. Charley hopes to apply his experience and knowledge to building a product that can make a big splash in the tech world and change the way people live.
